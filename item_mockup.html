Syema de Pon

2016-Q1 | Ruby, Gosu, MS Paint, Photoshop

A puzzle game written in Ruby using the Gosu Development Library. Based on classic puzzle games like its namesake, Panel de Pon, players must create horizontal or vertical words to clear letter blocks before they reach the top of the screen.
